# **GYN & PLACENTA PATH MASTER — OPTIMIZED MASTER PROMPT FOR EDUCATIONAL APP GENERATION**

A **mobile-first**, **dark-mode**, **offline-ready**, pathology-resident–level educational platform focused on **Gynecologic Pathology + Placental Pathology** exactly as learned in a **GYN pathology rotation**, **OB-GYN tumor signout rotation**, or standard texts (e.g., Kurman/WHO Classification; Benirschke for Placenta — conceptual only).

This app must teach:
- Cervical, endometrial, ovarian, vulvar, vaginal pathology  
- Gestational trophoblastic disease (GTD)  
- Benign vs atypical vs malignant glandular and squamous lesions  
- Hormonal/reactive patterns  
- Ovarian tumor classification (epithelial, germ cell, sex cord–stromal)  
- Endometrial hyperplasia vs carcinoma  
- Placental pathology (villous, maternal/fetal vascular malperfusion, infection, implantation lesions)  
- High-level IHC & morphomolecular concepts  
- Pitfalls, mimics, and integrated case reasoning  

All material is **synthetic**, **educational only**, **non-clinical**, **icon-based** (NO real histology).  
Assessments are **session-only**, with **no score tracking**.
